Advertising Campaign

Dick's Sporting Goods

The Challenge

Develop a series of promotional graphics that communicate motivation, performance, and consistency while aligning with an established athletic retail brand — creating visually engaging layouts that highlight products and inspire action while maintaining strong hierarchy across multiple formats.

The Solution

A cohesive set of promotional graphics combining bold typography, energetic imagery, and clear messaging to encourage confidence and commitment in training — adapted across print, social, email, and web formats.

CategoryAdvertising Campaign
Formats
PrintSocialEmailWeb
ToolsAdobe Illustrator · Photoshop · InDesign
01 — Print Advertisements

Full-Page Print Ads

Four full-page print ads built for retail placement — bold headline hierarchy paired with product imagery drives motivation and a clear call to action across each layout. Each ad targets a distinct message: Train Smarter, Built for Performance, Show Up. Keep Going., and Consistency Starts Here. Click the image to browse all four ads.

02 — Instagram · Pinterest · Email Blasts

Social Media & Email Campaign

A full digital campaign spanning Instagram feed posts (2160×2160px, exported at 1080×1080px), Pinterest vertical pins (2000×3000px, 2:3 ratio), and four email formats at 600px wide. The Instagram and Pinterest assets carry the same bold visual energy as the print ads — adapted for each platform's format and scroll behavior. The email series covers four formats: a promotional e-blast, a product-focused e-blast, an engagement e-blast, and a long-form newsletter — each with a single clear call to action designed to drive click-through. Click the image to browse all ten assets in order.

03 — Web Banner

Digital Display

Horizontal web banner built for homepage and landing page placements — "Train Smarter · Performance Starts Here" with a bold headline, italic subheading, and a high-contrast Shop Now CTA. Designed at full bleed width to command attention above the fold, with the Dick's Sporting Goods logo anchored top-left for immediate brand recognition. Click to open full size.
