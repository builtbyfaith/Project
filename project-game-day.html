Sports Marketing Campaign

Game Day

The Challenge

Create high-impact promotional graphics for basketball games that capture attention quickly and communicate essential game information through a clear visual hierarchy — built to work across print, social, and out-of-home formats.

The Solution

Bold Game Day posters using strong typography, dramatic lighting, and warm color contrast to create energy and movement. The design system was adapted across multiple digital formats to demonstrate consistency and versatility for real-world campaign use.

01 — Game Day Posters

Arizona Wildcats & Phoenix Suns

Two distinct Game Day poster designs built for high-impact sports promotion — each tailored to its team's visual identity while sharing a common design language of oversized typography, player cutouts, and bold hierarchy. Click either poster to open it full size.

Arizona Wildcats — Bear Down

View full size

A gritty, high-energy poster for the Arizona Wildcats featuring Brayden Burries (#5) — bold red stacked GAMEDAY typography repeats behind the player cutout on a dark distressed background. Event details (Friday, March 20th 2026 · McKale Center) and the Wildcats vs ASU matchup are anchored at the top and bottom. Shown as a physical print mockup to demonstrate real-world presence.

Phoenix Suns — Suns vs Lakers

View full size

A bold Phoenix Suns Game Day poster featuring oversized "GAME DAY" typography in warm off-white on a deep brown gradient — the player breaks out of the letterforms to create depth and movement. "SUNS vs LAKERS" is set in orange below the player, with the game details (April 18 · 7:00 PM) in clean white. The warm palette and dramatic lighting create instant visual impact.
