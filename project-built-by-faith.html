Brand Identity

Built by Faith

The Challenge

Create a typographic brand piece that communicates a personal identity in a clear and visually compelling way — without relying on imagery. The brand needed to express discipline, confidence, and resilience through type alone.

The Solution

A bold typographic composition built on the phrase "Built by Faith" — using repetition for rhythm, strong hierarchy to anchor the focal point, and a minimalist pink palette that lets the typography carry all the visual impact.

CategoryBrand Identity
Deliverables
Logo SystemBusiness CardsPrintApparel

01 — Brand System

Color Palette, Typography & Logo

The visual foundation of Built by Faith — a five-color pink palette anchored in near-black, a four-font type system, and six logo variations built to work across every surface. Click either image to open it full size.

Color Palette & Typography

View full size

Five PMS colors — hot pink, near-black, white, blush, and peach — create a palette that reads feminine and bold in equal measure. The type system pairs Inter for body copy, Arial Rounded MT Bold for headlines, Arial Black for the primary logo mark, and Allura Regular as a flowing script accent.

Logo System — 6 Variations

View full size

Six logo variations cover the full range of use cases — two classic wordmarks pairing Arial Black with the Allura script (one in black, one in pink), a reversed white-on-dark version, and three bold all-caps stacked lockups in pink, black, and dark-field, each paired with the tagline "commitment and confidence."

02 — Typographic Poster

In-Context Brand Display

The brand poster shown at gym scale on a concrete column — demonstrating real-world environmental presence. The bold all-caps stacked "BUILT BY FAITH" lockup dominates the center of a dark-field poster, flanked top and bottom by repeating pink marquee text. The tagline "commitment and confidence" runs in a light spaced treatment below the primary headline, reinforcing the brand voice at large format. Click to open full size.

03 — Business Cards & Print Assets

Collateral System

A complete print collateral suite — twelve business card variations and five stationery pieces — all built on the same brand mark and minimal layout language. Click either image to open it full size.

Business Cards — 12 Variations

View full size

Twelve designs across six front-and-back concepts — exploring weight, color blocking, and typographic hierarchy in landscape format. Backs consistently carry hello@builtbyfaith.co, builtbyfaith.co, and the tagline "commitment and confidence" in the pink Allura script.

Print Assets — Stationery System

View full size

Five stationery pieces on a consistent white ground with restrained pink accents — two letterhead formats, a full-page branded paper with a pink left sidebar and ghost "faith" watermark, and a thank-you card set with a large Allura script front and the brand message on the reverse.

04 — Apparel

Brand Applied to Product

The brand system applied across a full activewear and accessories line — demonstrating real-world versatility on product. A pink sports bra and leggings set carries the white wordmark on the back. A sand hoodie features the bold all-caps pink stacked lockup with "commitment and confidence" below. White and black trucker and dad cap variations show the brand at front and three-quarter angle. White joggers carry the minimal two-line "BUILT BY / faith" script treatment at the hip. Every piece confirms the brand holds across fabric, color, and scale. Click to open full size.
